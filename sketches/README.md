# Interactive Concepts Sketches

## Concept 1: Interactive Timeline

**Questions to Explore:**
When does certain topics surge or fade?

**About**
Adds a timeline slider (1770–1810) that lets users explore how dominant topics shift over decades.
Active years appear at full opacity while others fade, turning the mosaic view into a visual pulse of history.

## Concept 2: Interactive Map

**Questions to Explore:**
Which cities were the centers of political or social discourse?

**About**
A mid-landing interactive map where each circle represents a topic’s activity in a specific city and year.
Circle size corresponds to the number of documents, and color follows the topic palette.
As users scroll through time, circles appear and fade — visualizing how language spread geographically.

![Concept 2 Sketch](/major-studio-1/sketches/2.jpg)

## Concept 3: Interactive Text Explorer

**Questions to Explore:**
What related terms define each topic’s context?

**About**
In the detail view, hovering over highlighted words opens a small tooltip that summarizes the word’s topic, nearby co-occurring terms, and other sentences where it appears.
This reveals how multiple ideas coexist and interact within a single document.
